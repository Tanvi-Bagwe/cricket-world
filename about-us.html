Welcome to Cricket World

I am a cricket lover who believe the game is more than just a sport - it’s a passion, a culture, and for many of us, a way of life.

My Mission

My mission is simple - to bring cricket fans closer to the game. Whether you're looking for team rankings, highlights, or stunning visuals, Cricket World is designed to keep you informed and inspired.

What You'll Find Here

  • Home – Information about top ICC cricket teams
  • Rankings – Check top teams and players across formats.
  • Photo & Video Highlights – Relive recent match moments.

Why We Built This

I created Cricket World as part of a web design project - but also as a tribute to the game I love. I hope this platform adds value to your cricket journey!
